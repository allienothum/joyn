project description

The Interactive Design Capstone course at Maryville University challenges senior students to develop and produce a significant visual communication design project that culminates the skills, knowledge and understanding acquired throughout the program. The resulting project is expected to be multifaceted, intelligent, innovative, and purposeful.

project statement

JOYN is a multi­-platform application created to train the mind to become
more positive. By utilizing science­-based training exercises and promoting social
connections, JOYN helps users create a more fulfilling and joyful life.

project goals

  • utilize sound, sceintific research
  • avoid gimmickry
  • avoid stereotypical "happy go-lucky" attitude
  • help retrain the mindset to become
    more instinctively optimistic
  • help change thoughts, behaviors and actions
    to increase unification and positivity in relationship
